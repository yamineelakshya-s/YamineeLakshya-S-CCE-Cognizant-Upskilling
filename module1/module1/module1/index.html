Community Bulletin

📰 Latest Community News

The annual music festival returns this summer with over 20 local artists performing across three stages. Tickets are now available at the community center and online portal. Early bird discounts end this Friday.

Our food festival saw record attendance last year, and organizers are planning an even bigger event this season. Over 40 food stalls from local vendors will be set up at the central park grounds.

The youth sports league is accepting registrations for the upcoming season. Teams for football, cricket, and basketball will be formed by the end of the month. Coaches and volunteers are also welcome.

Cultural programs have been scheduled throughout the year, celebrating the diversity of our community. Traditional dance, art exhibitions, and heritage walks are all part of the exciting lineup.
